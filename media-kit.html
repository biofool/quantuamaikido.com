book description

Short (50 words)

What if conflict wasn't something to win or lose, but energy waiting to be transformed? Richard Moon bridges quantum physics with Aikido philosophy, showing how the principles governing subatomic particles govern human consciousness—offering a path to transform reaction into creative response.

Medium (100 words)

Quantum Aikido: The Power of Harmony reveals how the same principles governing subatomic particles govern human consciousness. Quantum physics transformed science by recognizing that the observer and active observation affects the experiment. Quantum Aikido transforms life by recognizing that the quality of engagements transforms the world. Richard Moon, 6th Dan Aikido sensei and student of martial arts and yoga for over 60 years, introduces core concepts: WIGO (What Is Really Going On), the Quantum Pause, the Observer Effect, and standing on the floating bridge where opposites complete each other. This is intellectually rigorous consciousness transformation for practitioners ready for sophisticated work.

Long (250 words)

In Quantum Aikido: The Power of Harmony, Richard Moon—6th Dan Aikido sensei and student of martial arts and yoga for over 60 years—reveals a revolutionary understanding: the principles governing subatomic particles also govern human consciousness and conflict.

Quantum physics transformed science by recognizing that the observer and active observation affects the experiment. Quantum Aikido transforms life by recognizing that the quality of engagements transforms the world. Through Quantum Aikido, discord informs the movement towards symphonic resonance, bringing the universe of an individual into balance and emanating those reciprocating echoes into the larger field of reality and creation.

Moon has worked in Cyprus, Bosnia, and around the Pacific Rim sharing these principles to help people create a beautiful world. He has also worked with leading corporations to help leaders and teams utilize the power of harmony for customer satisfaction and business success. Core concepts include WIGO (What Is Really Going On beneath surface appearances), the Quantum Pause (the space between stimulus and response where infinite possibility exists), and the Unified Field Experience (center and circle creating wholeness).

As O Sensei, the founder of Aikido, said: all the problems in this world exist because people have forgotten that everything emanates from a single source. Feelings of discomfort and unpleasant sensations, when understood through the power of harmony, guide one back to the source of power—the original source of creation.

Published by Inner Traditions/Park Street Press in the Sacred Planet Books series, with foreword by Douglas Stone (Harvard Law School lecturer, co-author of Difficult Conversations), Quantum Aikido offers intellectually rigorous, practically applicable wisdom for transforming how we meet pressure, conflict, change, and the unknown.

press release

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Quantum Aikido Reveals How Physics Principles Transform Human Conflict Into Creative Harmony

6th Dan Aikido Master and Peace Negotiator Richard Moon Bridges Ancient Wisdom with Modern Science in New Book Releasing January 6, 2026

What if conflict wasn't something to win or lose, but energy waiting to be transformed? This question drives Richard Moon's revolutionary new book, Quantum Aikido: The Power of Harmony (Inner Traditions/Park Street Press, January 6, 2026).

Moon, a 6th Dan Aikido sensei and student of martial arts and yoga for over 60 years, has worked in Cyprus, Bosnia, and around the Pacific Rim sharing the principles of Quantum Aikido to help people work together to create a beautiful world. Drawing from quantum mechanics—wave-particle duality, the observer effect, superposition—and Aikido philosophy, Moon offers a framework for transforming reactivity into creative response.

The book introduces core concepts including WIGO (What Is Really Going On beneath surface appearances), the Quantum Pause (the space between stimulus and response where infinite possibility exists), and the Unified Field Experience (center and circle creating wholeness).

"Aikido founder Morihei Ueshiba called his art 'the realization of love,'" Moon explains. "O Sensei taught that the sword of the Aiki spirit cuts things together rather than apart. That's the essence of this work—recognizing that conflict isn't an enemy to defeat but energy to harmonize."

Moon studied with Robert Nadeau (personal student of O Sensei), Bira Almeida (Mestre Acordeon, three-time World Capoeira Heavyweight Champion), and Peter Ralston (world champion martial artist and teacher of enlightenment). He also trained for over six years in music theater, improv theatre, and dance, applying these principles to leadership development and international peace relations.

Quantum Aikido: The Power of Harmony is published in the Sacred Planet Books series. Douglas Stone, Harvard Law School lecturer and co-author of Difficult Conversations, wrote the foreword.
